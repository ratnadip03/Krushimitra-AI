# 📖 Overview

KrishiMitra AI is a **Centralized Farmer Intelligence Platform** designed to empower Indian farmers with AI-powered decision support throughout the complete agricultural lifecycle.

Unlike traditional agricultural applications, every module works independently while sharing information through a **Centralized Farmer Brain**, enabling personalized recommendations without repeated data entry.

The platform supports both **online and offline AI**, making it suitable for rural areas with poor or no internet connectivity.

# 🚀 Features

## 🌱 Layer 1 — Soil & Crop Intelligence

### 🌿 Soil Health Passport & NPK Advisory
Generate Soil Health Score from OCR-based soil reports and recommend fertilizers.

### 🌾 Pre-Crop Soil Suitability Advisor
Suggest the best crops based on soil quality, season and water availability.

### 🔄 AI Crop Rotation Planner
Recommend optimal multi-season crop rotation plans.

---

## 🍅 Layer 2 — Harvest, Storage & Freshness

### 🥬 Produce Freshness & Shelf-Life Predictor
Predict freshness and remaining shelf life using AI image analysis.

### 📦 Smart Produce Storage Guide
Recommend ideal storage conditions for every produce type.

### 🌤 Harvest Timing AI
Suggest the best harvesting period using crop maturity and weather.

### ❄ Micro Cold Chain Advisor
Recommend nearby cold storage facilities with cost-benefit analysis.

---

## 📈 Layer 3 — Market Intelligence

### 💰 Best Time to Sell Advisor
Recommend the ideal selling time based on freshness and market trends.

### 📊 AI Market Price Prediction
Forecast mandi prices and assist with negotiation strategies.

---

## ♻️ Layer 4 — Food Waste Management

### 📉 Post-Harvest Loss Intelligence
Predict storage and transportation losses before they occur.

### 🤝 Surplus Crop Matching Exchange
Connect farmers with NGOs, FPOs, food banks and processors.

### 🏭 Value Addition Advisor
Suggest processing options such as pickles, powders and dried products.

### 🌿 Agri-Waste Revenue Engine
Convert agricultural waste into compost, biogas, biochar and animal feed.

---

## 🌍 Layer 5 — Sustainability & Government Support

### 🌎 Farm-to-Table Carbon Tracker
Track carbon emissions across the agricultural lifecycle.

### 🏛 Smart Government Scheme Eligibility Engine
Recommend government schemes and subsidies based on farmer profile.

---

## 🎤 Layer 6 — Accessibility

### 🗣 Offline-First Multilingual Voice Assistant

Supports:

- 🇮🇳 Marathi
- 🇮🇳 Hindi
- 🇬🇧 English

Works in both Online and Offline mode.
